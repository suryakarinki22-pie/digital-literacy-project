# Social Media Do's and Don'ts 

## Do's

1. Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles clean and professional
2. Think before posting — would you be okay if your professor saw it?
3. Use strong, unique passwords for each social media account
4. Enable privacy settings to control who sees your posts
5. Give credit when sharing someone else's content or work
6. Use social media to learn — follow developers, educators and industry experts

## Don'ts

1. Don't share your address, phone number, or location publicly
2. Don't post angry or emotional content during a conflict
3. Don't click on unknown links or suspicious messages
4. Don't accept follow or friend requests from strangers
5. Don't spread news without verifying it first
6. Don't use the same password across multiple platforms
