# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
---

1. Never share your OTP, PIN, or password with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or government.

2. Do not click on random links sent through SMS, WhatsApp, or email. Always verify before opening.

3. Use strong passwords and turn on two-step verification on your Gmail, Instagram, and all important apps.

4. Never install apps like AnyDesk or TeamViewer if someone on a call asks you to. This gives them full access to your phone.

5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for any banking or UPI transactions. Use mobile data instead.

6. Check the receiver's name carefully on UPI before confirming any payment. Scammers use fake IDs that look like official ones.

7. You do NOT need to enter your PIN to receive money on UPI. If someone says you do, they are stealing from you.

8. Set a daily transaction limit on your UPI app like PhonePe or Google Pay to reduce loss in case of fraud.

9. Do not post your phone number, Aadhaar number, or bank details on any social media platform.

10. Keep your phone and apps updated regularly. Old versions have security gaps that scammers misuse.

---

## If You Are a Victim

- Report online at: **cybercrime.gov.in**
- Call the 24x7 helpline: **1930** (free, Government of India)
- Immediately call your bank and ask them to freeze your account.
- Save all screenshots and call recordings as proof.
