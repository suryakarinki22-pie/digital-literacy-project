# Task 1 – Infographic Brief Description

## Tool Used
Canva

## What My Design Shows

I created a one-page infographic titled "5 Pillars of Digital Literacy"
using Canva. The infographic covers all 5 key topics required for this
task:

1. What is Digital Literacy – explains the ability to find, evaluate,
   and communicate information responsibly across digital platforms

2. Useful Digital Tools – highlights platforms that help students
   succeed, from simulation software to creative applications

3. Safe Internet Practices – covers protecting identity and data from
   cyber threats, verifying links, and using strong passwords

4. Professional Online Presence – explains the importance of building
   a clean and visible digital profile for academic and career growth

5. Email Etiquette – describes how to communicate professionally with
   professors and recruiters using proper subject lines and greetings

The design uses a bold blue and yellow color scheme to make the content
visually engaging and easy to read.

## One Thing I Found Interesting

The most interesting part was learning how to arrange information in a
visual format. I found it challenging to fit all the content into one
page while keeping it clean and readable. Using Canva's pre-built
layouts helped me understand how good design can make information more
accessible and impactful.
